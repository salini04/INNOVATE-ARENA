Understanding Your Heart 🫀

Learn how your heart works and protect it for life

🫀 Overview of the Heart

Structure

The heart has four chambers: two atria and two ventricles working together.

Function

It pumps oxygen-rich blood to all organs and keeps the body alive.

Power

Beats over 100,000 times a day without stopping.

🔄 How the Heart Works

Blood follows this path:

Body → Right Heart → Lungs (oxygen) → Left Heart → Body

This continuous loop ensures oxygen reaches every cell in your body.
